Finding your server credentials

Before you can begin with this tutorial, you need to know the credentials for your servers. The main server that you will SSH into is your Master Node and you'll look up the root password by its IP address: {{cluster_data.master_node_ip}} this can be found on your cluster's Guidance Page

Once you know your Master Node IP address, please log in to your GoGrid console at my.gogrid.com with the credentials that you used when you set up your account. This will take you to a screen that shows all of the servers that are in your cluster.

Click on the Passwords button to see the passwords screen.

The passwords screen shows you the root passwords for the nodes in your cluster. The host that you will be logging in to is the Master Node (that you collected the IP address for).
